Far Cry 6 PC Version Supports Ray-Tracing and FidelityFX

How thick your wallet is, how much you want to spend on PC components, will certainly determine how powerful your current rig is. Its strength as a super sweet platform does come with its own advantages and disadvantages. Deficiency? Because there is no standardization of specifications as is the case with consoles, there is a possibility that certain combinations of components will result in sub-optimal performance. Excess? You can potentially play all games in the best of the best formats, as long as they are supported by the developer. This last business seems to be being prepared by Ubisoft.

Ubisoft has just released the latest Far Cry 6 trailer to introduce a myriad of features for the PC version later. Seemingly working closely with AMD, Far Cry 6 will come with hybrid ray-tracing that will simulate reflections and shadows more accurately. Powered by benchmarks to help you get better performance, Far Cry 6 PC will also support FidelityFX and vary in resolution depending on the number of monitors. Those of you who want even better visuals can also download HD Texture Pack for it.

Unfortunately, Ubisoft itself has not spoken about the official PC specifications required to play this game, at least in the lowest quality. Far Cry 6 will be released on October 7, 2021. How about you? Just looking forward to this one game?

Minimum Requirements (1080p 30fps LOW)

  • Operating System: Windows 10 (20H1 or Newer) 64bit
  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 3 1200 – 3.1 GHz / Intel i5-4460 – 3.2 GHz
  • GPU: AMD RX 460 4GB / NVIDIA GTX 960 4GB
  • RAM: 8 GB (Dual-Channel Mode)
  • Storage: 60 GB HDD (SSD Recommended)

Recommended Requirements (1080p 60fps HIGH)

  • Operating System: Windows 10 (20H1 or Newer) 64bit
  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 5 3600X – 3.8 GHz / Intel i7-9700K – 3.6 GHz
  • GPU: AMD RX 5700XT 8 GB / NVIDIA RTX 2070 Super 8 GB
  • AM: 16 GB (Dual-Channel Mode)
  • Storage: 60 GB HDD (SSD Recommended) + 37 GB HD Textures (Optional)
